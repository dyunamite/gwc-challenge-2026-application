Art Against AI

Welcome to Art Against AI!

We aim to help creators protect their work from being used to train AI models.

Upload your visual art, music, or writing, and we’ll apply subtle changes that preserve originality while disuading AI ingestion.

Our Approach

Visual Art Music Literature
Our research found that AI models struggle to replicacte continuous, unbroken patterns. Therefore, we use this concept in the form of a watermark to protect your art. We find areas with minimal visual impact and place the watermark there. Our research shows that AI systems rely on clean, consistent patterns to process and learn from audio. By subtly introducing controlled noise at the beginning and end of each track, we disrupt automated ingestion while preserving a seamless listening experience for your audience. Our research shows that AI systems depend on clean, uninterrupted text structures to interpret language accurately. By embedding zero-width characters within morphemes, we subtly disrupt machine parsing while keeping the text visually identical and fully readable to human audiences.
